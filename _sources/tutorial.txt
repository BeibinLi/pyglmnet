GLM with elastic net penalty
----------------------------
In Generalized Linear Models (GLMs), we want to solve the following problem.

.. math::
    \min_{\beta_0, \beta} \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i = 1}^N w_i \mathcal{L} (y_i, \beta_0 + \beta^T x_i)
    + \lambda [0.5(1 - \alpha)\| \beta \|_2^2 + \alpha \| \beta \|_1]

where :math:`\mathcal{L} (y_i, \beta_0 + \beta^T x_i)` is the negative log-likelihood of
an observation :math:`y_i` given the parameters :math:`\beta_0` and :math:`\beta`.
