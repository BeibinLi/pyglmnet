GLM with elastic net penalty¶

In Generalized Linear Models (GLMs), we want to solve the following problem.

\[\min_{\beta_0, \beta} \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i = 1}^N w_i \mathcal{L} (y_i, \beta_0 + \beta^T x_i) + \lambda [0.5(1 - \alpha)\| \beta \|_2^2 + \alpha \| \beta \|_1]\]

where \(\mathcal{L} (y_i, \beta_0 + \beta^T x_i)\) is the negative log-likelihood of an observation \(y_i\) given the parameters \(\beta_0\) and \(\beta\).
